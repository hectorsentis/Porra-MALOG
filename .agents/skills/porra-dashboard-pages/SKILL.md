# PORRA Dashboard Pages Skill

## Mandatory public pages

- `/`
- `/clasificacion`
- `/evolucion`
- `/apuestas`
- `/departamentos`
- `/simulador`
- `/participantes`
- `/participantes/[slug]`
- `/estadisticas`
- `/reglas`

## Home

Must include:

- KPIs: líder, puntos, distancia al segundo, participantes, puntos repartidos, partidos computados, última actualización.
- Ranking resumido.
- Top 10.
- Composición de puntos.
- Movimientos.
- Media por departamento.

## Clasificación

Full sortable table, search, filters, gaps, CSV export and participant card.

## Evolución

Position evolution, points evolution, points by matchday, best/worst matchday.

## Apuestas

Champion, runner-up, top scorer, team markets, differential bets, popular vs rare.

## Departamentos

Average ranking, total ranking, MVP, distribution and internal ranking.

## Simulador

Match simulator, champion simulator, projected ranking, potential points, alive participants, hot matches.

## Participantes

Directory and individual profile.

## Estadísticas

Histogram, ranking density, hits by type, consistency, volatility and consensus markets.

## Visual identity

- Title: PORRA MUNDIAL 2026 MALOG.
- Air Force/MALOG theme.
- Clean, compact, responsive.
- No Excel-looking default colors.
